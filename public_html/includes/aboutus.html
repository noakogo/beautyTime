About Us

Beauty Time is a boutique spa that offers you to come and escape from your routine and daily life and
come to be pampered with our treatments and products that made with love especially for you.
Next to the largest park in the Ono Valley, between cafes and shops, we have created a place that will
allow you to take a moment for yourself, a short break between work and errands, between parenting and career.
Whether you're having a fun day with friends or running around between meetings. You do deserve to grab a peaceful corner in the
flow of the daily routine, reboot yourself, fill up with energy and come out fresh and renewed.
At Beauty Time you can rest and relax, enjoy pleasant music, an addictive smell and a relaxing atmosphere.
You will meet a professional team with experience and knowledge in all areas of beauty awaits you, which will offer you
a variety of treatments and accompany you to the perfect result! All this, by using the best materials currently
on the market (O.P.I, CND, LAYLA MILANO, ESSIE and more).
